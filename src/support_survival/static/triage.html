Triage panel

Decision-support risk stratification, not automated resource allocation.

Read before using this panel This ranks a fixed sample of patients from a historical 1990s research cohort, not a live hospital feed — a demonstration, not a deployed clinical tool. Discrimination is markedly worse for patients 85+ (AUROC ~0.60 vs ~0.74 for under-50s) — treat risk estimates for the oldest patients as lower-confidence. Tiers are meant to prompt a care-team conversation (e.g. a goals-of-care discussion), never to trigger an automatic decision.
